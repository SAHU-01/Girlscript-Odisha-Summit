Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Google Crowdsource?

With Google's new Android app, you can help the company translate languages, understand handwriting and keep its shareholders happy. Google Crowdsource lets you help the company with language translation, handwriting recognition and map translation accuracy.

How do I become a Contributor?

All you need to do is head out to Play Store and download the crowdsource application and start contributing to any feature on app, you'll become a contributor!

What is Girlscript Foundation?

GirlScript Foundation is a section-8 NON PROFIT company registered under the government of India on 2nd June 2017.GirlScript hosts many tech events and coding competition to help you get started and learn computer programming.
